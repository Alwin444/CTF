For /etc/shadow craks:  unshadow passwd shadow > hashes.txt
			john hashes.txt --wordlist=/usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt

If you extract a file but it's password protected: zip2john secret.zip > ziphash.txt
						   john ziphash.txt

SSH Private Key with Passphrase(id_rsa): ssh2john id_rsa > keyhash.txt
					 john keyhash.txt

For Password protected pdf and word file: pdf2john flag.pdf > pdfhash.txt
					  john pdfhash.txt
